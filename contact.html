Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ReServe

How do I donate items?

You can donate items through our website by filling out the donation form. We accept both food and clothing donations. Simply select the type of donation you want to make and follow the step-by-step process.

What items can I donate?

We accept gently used clothing in good condition and non-perishable food items that are within their expiration date. All items should be clean and properly packaged.

How do I schedule a pickup?

When you submit a donation form, you can select your preferred pickup date and time. Our team will contact you to confirm the pickup and provide any additional instructions.

Can I volunteer with ReServe?

Yes! We welcome volunteers to help with sorting donations, organizing events, and supporting our community outreach programs. Contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.

How do I earn rewards points?

You earn points for every donation you make. The more you donate, the more points you accumulate. These points can be redeemed for various rewards and recognition within our community.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to ReServe are tax-deductible. We provide donation receipts for all contributions, which you can use for tax purposes.
