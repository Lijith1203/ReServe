Donation Dos & Don'ts

Help us make a positive impact by following these simple guidelines for food and clothing donations.

Food Donation

Dos

  • Donate non-perishable, unopened, and properly sealed food items.
  • Check expiration dates and only donate food that is within date.
  • Include ingredient and allergen information if possible.
  • Ensure packaging is clean and undamaged.
  • Wash your hands before handling donations.

Don'ts

  • Do not donate expired or spoiled food.
  • Avoid donating homemade or partially used items.
  • Do not include food with damaged, bulging, or rusted cans.
  • Avoid items without clear labeling or ingredient info.
  • Do not donate perishable items unless specifically requested.

Clothes Donation

Dos

  • Wash and clean all clothes before donating.
  • Donate gently used, undamaged, and wearable items.
  • Sort and fold clothes neatly.
  • Include shoes, accessories, and seasonal items in good condition.
  • Check pockets for personal items before donating.

Don'ts

  • Do not donate dirty, stained, or torn clothing.
  • Avoid donating undergarments unless new and packaged.
  • Do not include items with strong odors or pet hair.
  • Avoid donating single shoes or socks.
  • Do not donate items with missing buttons or broken zippers.
